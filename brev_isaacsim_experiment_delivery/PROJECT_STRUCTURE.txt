This structure may evolve slightly as implementation proceeds, but the core components (setup scripts, source modules, configs, notebooks, data logs, figures and report) should remain consistent.  Any additional files created during experimentation (e.g. policy checkpoints, demonstration recordings) should be placed under `runs/` or an appropriate subdirectory and noted in the README and final report.
